About

Click For More Info
The Binghamton University High-Performance Computing Cluster aptly name “Spiedie” is a 3688 compute core & 512 Knights Landing core cluster housed in the Thomas J. Watson School of Engineering and Applied Science's data center located at the Innovative Technology Complex. This research facility offers computer capabilities for researchers across Binghamton University.
